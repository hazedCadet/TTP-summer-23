Agua de Jamaica
Agua de Tamarindo
Agua de Horchata
jamaica recipe
  • boil flowers for 3 -5 mins
  • strain flowers
  • cool the tea
  • add sugar to taste
 tamarindo recipe
  • shell tamarinds
  • boil tamarinds for 2 mins
  • steep for 2 hours
  • strain tea and pulp into pitcher
  • add sugar to taste
 horchata recipe
  • put rice and cinammon in bowl
  • cover with 4 cups hot water
  • after 24 hours blend the mixture
  • strain and add sugar to taste
  • optional: add milk
  • optional: add vanilla
